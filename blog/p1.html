AGENCY AUTO

AgencyAuto provides in-depth analytical reports. Custom report engine, live dashboard, summary reports, one click PPT, and PDF makes life easy for the MIS team. AgencyAuto tracks all the transactions and gives summarized report for BSP reconciliation that helps in smooth flow of data and funds between travel agencies and airlines to ensure better control. AgencyAuto helps travel agencies to set up policies for each corporate such that every request raised is cross-checked to ensure non-violation of company policies. AgencyAuto allows travel agencies to map different coupons and offers for different customers which automatically gets displayed at the time of booking. AgencyAuto can be seamlessly integrated with multiple GDS system, accounting, and HRMS software.

AgencyAuto systematically checks every PNR at the time of booking and helps in the internal communication that prevents PNR re-work. AgencyAuto helps travel agencies to provide a self-booking portal to the corporates that automate travel planning, requisition, approval, fulfillment, invoicing and reporting. Our travel technology specialist are constantly on the move, to provide the best solution to our client.
